About

I am a videographer, editor and animator currently located in New Jersey.

After studying cinema production at Ithaca College and graduating with honors, I spent five years working in Hollywood before returning to the East Coast.

Since 2010, I have primarily been working with non-profits and academic institutions to create visual multimedia content for the web.

Most recently, at the Rutgers Film Bureau, I worked on two feature length documentaries. While there, I served as Editor, Animator and Associate Producer on "Antarctic Edge: 70° South", a climate change documentary funded by the National Science Foundation, and a Camera Operator for "The War After: Combat to Campus".

In 2013, I also taught an introductory editing course as part of the Rutgers Center for Digital Filmmaking's undergraduate certificate program.
