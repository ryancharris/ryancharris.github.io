About

As you’ve might’ve guessed from the oversized banner above, I’m Ryan.

But what you might not know is that I am a seasoned multimedia specialist and burgeoning front-end web developer currently living in Portland, Maine.

After accepting a writing position in early 2015, I moved from N.J. to Portland in order to help develop and create a daily, online satirical news show.

Prior to relocating, I spent four years working at Rutgers University creating multimedia content for a wide variety of departments.

At the Rutgers Film Bureau, I worked on two feature length documentaries, including “Antarctic Edge: 70° South”, a climate change documentary funded by the National Science Foundation. At the same time, I also taught an introductory video editing course as part of the Rutgers Center for Digital Filmmaking’s undergraduate certificate program.

Before that, I studied cinema production at Ithaca College, graduated with honors and spent five years working in Hollywood.
